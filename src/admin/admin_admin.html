Vision

A safe, healthy, and progressive community where residents can live and work with dignity and opportunity.

Mission

To provide efficient and effective public services, promote community development, and ensure the well-being of all residents through collaborative efforts and sustainable practices.

Goals

  • Safety and Security: Ensuring a safe environment for residents through effective law enforcement and community initiatives.
  • Health and Well-being: Promoting health and wellness through access to healthcare, sanitation, and public health programs.
  • Community Development: Fostering economic opportunities, education, and social development through various initiatives.
  • Good Governance: Implementing transparent and accountable governance practices to ensure public trust.
  • Environmental Sustainability: Protecting and preserving the environment through responsible resource management and conservation.
